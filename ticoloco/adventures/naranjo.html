Naranjo

Our Naranjo River Adventure is a half-day tour that begins with a pick-up at your hotel or private residence. As we head 30 minutes outside of Quepos/Manuel Antonio, we'll describe the plants and animals you may encounter in this region as well as go over safety procedures. At the entrance, youâ€™re introduced to a very technical CLASS III and IV set of rapids that are guaranteed to bring excitement to novice and experienced rafters alike. A light snack of local fruit, cookies along with water and juice is provided as you exit the river. This is a CLASS III and IV river and all ages are welcome.

Time and Details

  • Pick-up at 8:00 AM or 1:00 PM
  • 3 1/2 hour duration

What is Included

  • Round trip transportation
  • Driving tour through rainforest
  • Bilingual tour guide
  • Safety instruction & safety personnel
  • Fresh fruit snack
  • Water

What Should I Bring

  • Clothing that can get wet and/or bathing suit
  • Change of clothes
  • Towel
  • Sturdy shoes (no flip flops/sandals)
  • Sunblock & bug spray
  • Additional water is also recommended
