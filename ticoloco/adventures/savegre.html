Savegre

Our Savegre River adventure starts with a pick-up at your hotel or private residence. As we set out for the day, we will provide fun facts about the local villages we pass thru and the people that live in them. We’ll trek through the palm fields and up into the mountains, as we find our entrance to Costa Rica's cleanest river. Once in the water, we’ll instantly meet some of the rivers Class II & III rapids while enjoying stunning 360 degree views of lush mountain rainforest. Halfway down river we will stop off for breathtaking hidden waterfall which offers a refreshing swimming hole at the base. The waterfall is the perfect spot to enjoy some fresh fruit and cookies along with juice and water provide by Tico Loco Adventures. As we return back to Manuel Antonio, there is an option for a stop at a local hot spot for lunch. This is a CLASS II and III river and all ages are welcome.

Time and Details

  • Pick-up at 8:00 AM
  • 6 hour duration

What is Included

  • Round trip transportation
  • Driving tour through rainforest
  • Bilingual tour guide
  • Safety instruction & safety personnel
  • Fresh fruit snack
  • Traditional Costa Rican lunch
  • Water

What Should I Bring

  • Clothing that can get wet and/or bathing suit
  • Change of clothes
  • Towel
  • Sturdy shoes (no flip flops/sandals)
  • Sunblock & bug spray
  • Additional water is also recommended
